**Line 13** – `VisibilityModifier` / **Field should be private and accessed via constructor or setter**: `toGraphicIfPresent` has package-private effective visibility risk, but more precisely both fields lack `private final` — they are mutable instance fields set outside the constructor, violating **Immutable Field** / **Field Not Final**.
